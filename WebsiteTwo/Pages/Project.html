This is the page for my projects. Since this was my first time working with Adobe platforms, I’ll do my best to explain concisely how to create a similar project to my example.

*** It is important to note that the time spent before creating these projects dealt with clicking on random buttons to see what they can do.This will help familiarize yourself to the platform. I highly encourage this technique that I call experimenting. The rest of the time was spent watching tutorials and painstakingly attempting to recreate the examples in the videos.

To begin, when you are envisioning your ideas, you should talk about them, write them out, draw it, and think about how you want the final product to look (Brainstorm). Furthermore, keep in mind that although you have an envisioned piece you want to create, don’t be concrete with the image, because you are a beginner and may not know how to create it yet.
